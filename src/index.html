About Me

As a recent graduate with a Master of Science in Computer Science from the University at Buffalo, I possess a passion for problem-solving and a strong foundation in software development. I am confident in my ability to make a valuable contribution to any forward-thinking organization in the technology industry.


Throughout my academic career, I have gained valuable experience working on cross-functional teams to create innovative solutions. I am proficient in programming languages such as Python, JavaScript and Java, as well as software tools. Additionally, I possess strong analytical and communication skills that enable me to effectively collaborate with colleagues and clients alike.


My ultimate goal is to leverage my education and experience to contribute to the growth and success of a technology organization. I am highly motivated to continuously learn and incorporate new technologies and methodologies into my work, and I am excited to take on new challenges and become a valuable asset to any team in the technology industry.


My technical proficiencies include programming languages such as Python, Java, JavaScript, C++, C, HTML, CSS, and PHP. I have experience working with databases such as MySQL, PostgreSQL, NoSQL, MongoDB, and SQLite. I am also proficient in frameworks/tools/cloud such as Node.js, Spring, Hibernate, Django, Flask, React.js, AngularJS, Vue.js, Redis, Apache Kafka, Mocha, JUnit, and AWS.


I have gained professional experience as a Full Stack Developer, where I developed and maintained websites using React, Redux, JavaScript, Java, Spring, Hibernate, HTML5, CSS3, and MySQL. I have also worked as an Apprentice at Persistent Systems, where I collaborated with a team of developers to design and implement a drone's control system and visual interface using Python and the A* algorithm for optimal pathfinding.


In addition to my professional experience, I have contributed to academic projects such as Pintos, where I developed various operating system features using C, GDB, and QEMU. I have also contributed to OneDataShare.org, an open-source data transfer software, and developed a full-stack web application called ReviewScope using ReactJS, Redux, NodeJS, and MongoDB to manage conference roles.


I am currently pursuing a Master of Science in Computer and Information Science at the University at Buffalo, where I also serve as a Graduate Teaching Assistant for CSE 4/521 Operating Systems.
