Hey there! I'm Prashanth, a Master of Science in Computer and Information Science student at the University at Buffalo. As a graduate teaching assistant for Operating Systems, I've discovered my passion for helping others and sharing my knowledge. My technical skills include proficiency in programming languages like Python, Java, JavaScript, C++, C, HTML, CSS, and PHP, and databases like MySQL, PostgreSQL, NoSQL, MongoDB, and SQLite. I'm also experienced in frameworks and tools like Node.js, Spring, Hibernate, Django, Flask, React.js, AngularJS, Vue.js, Mocha, JUnit, and AWS. But what sets me apart is my ability to work collaboratively with a team and my eagerness to learn new things. I've had the privilege of working as a Full Stack Developer at Hans Matrimony, where I developed and maintained EazyBe.com websites using React, Redux, JavaScript, Java, Spring, Hibernate, HTML5, CSS3, and MySQL. In addition to integrating multiple third-party APIs and building a comprehensive components library, I implemented client-side data caching and designed a microservices architecture to improve application scalability, performance, and availability. Outside of work and school, I'm a huge fan of reading, exploring the great outdoors, and spending time with my loved ones. I believe that being a well-rounded individual and fostering personal growth is just as important as technical expertise. Overall, I'm excited to continue growing and contributing to the field of computer science while maintaining a balanced and fulfilling life outside of work.
